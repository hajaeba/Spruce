Dallas College is a large community
college system with seven
campuses together

offering a wide variety of associate and bachelor's degrees and career certificates
Its student body is diverse, with a large Hispanic population,
and includes a significant number of dual credit, military-connected,
and transfer-out students The college has several "Schools of Instruction" that organize its diverse programs.
