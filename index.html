Dallas College is one of the largest public, multi-campus community college systems in Texas,
founded in 1965 to provide affordable higher education and workforce development to the Dallas area.
It offers a wide range of programs, including associate and bachelor's degrees, career certificates,
and customized training, spread across multiple physical campuses and online options.
The institution focuses on transforming lives and strengthening
the Dallas County community through accessible education
